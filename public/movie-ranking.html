The Best Movies According to Rhonda

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Fox and the Hound

A heartwarming tale of friendship between a young fox named Tod and a hound dog named Copper. Despite their natural instincts to be enemies, they form an unbreakable bond that teaches us about loyalty, acceptance, and the power of friendship.

Benny and Joon

A quirky romantic comedy about two eccentric individuals, Benny and Joon, who find love and companionship in each other's unique perspectives on life. Their journey is filled with humor, heart, and a touch of whimsy.

Boondock Saints

A gritty action film that follows fraternal twin brothers who become vigilantes, taking on the criminal underworld of Boston. Their journey raises questions about morality, justice, and the consequences of taking the law into their own hands.
